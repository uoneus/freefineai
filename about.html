Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about FreeFineAI plans.

Why upgrade from the free plan? +

Paid plans are designed for creators who need faster queues, watermark-free exports, more monthly credits, fewer interruptions, and cleaner commercial workflows.

Can I cancel or change plans? +

Yes. You can move between plans or cancel from your account settings. Your current plan remains active until the end of the billing period.

Can I use generated images commercially? +

Commercial usage depends on the plan, model, and your compliance with the FreeFineAI terms. Premium and Ultimate are intended for cleaner professional use.

Will a paid plan improve generation speed? +

Paid plans receive priority queue access. Ultimate receives the highest priority, which is helpful during peak traffic.

Do subscriptions include API access? +

Public API access is not included by default. For high-volume or custom integration needs, contact the FreeFineAI team to discuss an enterprise setup.
